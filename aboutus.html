ABOUT US

THE ONLY HOTEL WHERE HERITAGE MEETS elegance

A SANCTUARY OF luxury IN THE HEART OF THE CITY

Nestled in the vibrant cityscape of Singapore, Château Herâld stands as a testament to the island's rich history and modern sophistication. Originally established in the colonial era, this luxury hotel seamlessly blends classic heritage charm with contemporary elegance. Located just a stone's throw away from the iconic Marina Bay Sands and the historic Raffles Hotel, Château Harâld offers guests an unparalleled experience of opulence and comfort. Each suite is a sanctuary of refined taste, adorned with bespoke furnishings and breathtaking views of the city’s skyline. Indulge in our world-class dining options, where culinary artistry meets the diverse flavors of Singapore’s storied past. Whether you're unwinding in our rooftop infinity pool or exploring the nearby cultural landmarks, Château Harâld promises a journey through time in the lap of luxury. Experience the perfect harmony of Singapore’s vibrant history and cutting-edge luxury at Chateau Harald, where every stay is an unforgettable story waiting to be told.

MEET OUR TEAM

Harald Goh Jun Jie

Founder

Born and raised in Singapore, Harald's deep appreciation for his homeland's rich cultural tapestry and dynamic progress inspired him to create a hotel that embodies both heritage and modernity. With a background in international business and a passion for exquisite design, Harald meticulously crafted this luxury hotel to offer an unparalleled guest experience.

Emily St. Laurent

Executive Chef

Emily is the culinary mastermind behind Château Herald's renowned dining experiences. With over 20 years of experience, Emily has worked in prestigious kitchens around the globe, bringing a wealth of knowledge and creativity. Emily's commitment to using the finest ingredients have earned the hotel's restaurant numerous accolades and a loyal following of gastronomes.

Jonathan Lim Wei Sheng

General Manager

Jonathan Lim Wei Sheng brings over 15 years of hospitality management experience to his role as General Manager of Château Herald Singapore. With a background in both luxury and boutique hotel management, Jonathan excels in delivering exceptional guest experiences. His strategic vision and hands-on leadership style have been instrumental in maintaining the high standards of service that Château Herald is known for.

Rachel Ng Mei Ling

Director of Sales and Marketing

Rachel Ng Mei Ling is the driving force behind Château Herald's dynamic sales and marketing strategies. With a decade of experience in luxury hospitality marketing, Rachel has a talent for crafting compelling campaigns that resonate with discerning travelers. Rachel's innovative approach and her strong network within the industry ensure that the hotel remains a top choice for luxury travelers worldwide.
